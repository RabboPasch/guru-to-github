<h1 class="ghq-card-content__large-heading" data-ghq-card-content-type="LARGE_HEADING" id="2PzaUZa9dsdr">
 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)
</h1>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="SiV55jbkbkzH">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   1. What languages does the Wio Personal application support?
  </span>
 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="5I3V7CVRn3l7">
 The Wio Personal application supports two languages: English and Arabic. You can switch the app's language by changing your device's system language to Arabic and restarting the app.
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="HzRfHP7cTTgb">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   2. How can I update my email address or phone number in the Wio Personal app?
  </span>
 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="9WVbHk6jesnB">
 To update your email or phone number, log in to the Wio Personal application, navigate to settings, select your name, and choose the option to edit either your email address or phone number. Remember that both email and phone number can't be changed together within 24 hours.
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="hJGtLh5xd795">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   3. What should I do if I forget my passcode?
  </span>
 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="Hwe0BtkDPDOu">
 If you forget your passcode, you can reset it by opening the Wio Personal application, selecting "Forgot your passcode?", and following the two-step verification process, which includes entering an OTP and submitting a selfie video to verify your identity.
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="XcgjtvrjFxBr">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   4. How does device binding work in the Wio Personal application?
  </span>
 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="3FNIQXdr3vUP">
 Device binding ensures that your registered devices are trusted. When you set up a passcode during onboarding, it binds that device, unbinding any previously registered devices. Logging out of the app will manually unbind the device.
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="RFEP9YV0fBN5">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   5. What happens if there are multiple failed login attempts?
  </span>
 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="VYNTLJTryTGW">
 After 3 failed attempts using SMS/Email OTP, there will be a temporary lockout. Additionally, if 3 OTP requests are unused, there will be a 15-minute block on further requests. After 6 consecutive passcode failures, the account will be indefinitely blocked.
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="Jx98Zb3TVmjP">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   6. How can I update my Emirates ID or personal information?
  </span>
 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="hbZzrlFm5Blx">
 To update your Emirates ID, log in to the Wio Personal application, go to settings, select your name, and upload a clear photo of both sides of your Emirates ID. You‚Äôll also need to record a brief selfie video for identity verification.
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="DnTMYNl1vhjX">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   7. Is there any fee for closing my Wio Personal account?
  </span>
 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="D7TNxn1Tnff5">
 No, there are no fees for closing your Wio Personal account. You can close your account at any time without incurring any charges.
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="HlipNnrihRt1">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   8. Will¬†Wio mobile application work on IOS devices that are jailbroken?
  </span>
 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="ktetZ5hhr2dh">
 The Wio application does not support access on jailbroken iOS devices. The device management service used by Wio includes functionality to check if a device is rooted or jailbroken, and such devices are not allowed access to the Wio app
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="jUU1cZdMXXzB">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   9. Is it possible to change the account name in Wio personal?
  </span>
 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="MrPtZwDnZbvb">
 No, it is not possible to change the account name in Wio Personal. Each account name is defaulted to its related currency and cannot be modified.
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="qUAGFl6qDgIJ">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   10. How to hide the available account balance in Wio Personal App?
  </span>
 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="gzWGexQrYSKa">
 As a part of the enhanced privacy that the Wio Personal application is offering to its customers, to hide your balance from being displayed, you‚Äôll find an eye icon displayed on the home screen, next to your account balance. Tap it to hide or unhide your balance!
</p>
