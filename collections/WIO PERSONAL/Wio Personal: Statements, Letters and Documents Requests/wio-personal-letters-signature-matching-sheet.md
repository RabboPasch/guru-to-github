<h2 class="ghq-card-content__medium-heading" data-ghq-card-content-type="MEDIUM_HEADING">
 Topic Overview &amp; Definition of the Signature Matching Sheet
</h2>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph">
 The
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  Signature Matching Sheet or signature confirmation letter
 </strong>
 is a document that provides a specimen signature for customers, primarily used when they need to submit a police report. Customers can request this document, but it is not a requirement in all situations. To ensure proper handling and documentation, a specific format for the Signature Matching Sheet has been provided by the Legal Team, and assistance can be sought from support teams or Team Leaders when needed.
</p>
