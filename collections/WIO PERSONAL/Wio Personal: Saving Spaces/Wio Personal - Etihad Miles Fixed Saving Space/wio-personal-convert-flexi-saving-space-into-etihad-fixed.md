   </em>
  </strong>
 </summary>
 <div class="ghq-card-content__collapsible-content" data-ghq-card-content-type="COLLAPSIBLE_CONTENT">
  <p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph">
   You cannot add money to the Etihad Guest Fixed Saving Space or withdraw part of the deposit once it is opened.
   <br/>
   <br/>
   You can close the Etihad Guest Fixed Saving Space anytime and keep the Etihad Guest Miles you've received. However, in case of early closure, you'll need to compensate the remaining cost of the Etihad Guest Miles. This compensation is calculated as 0.5% of the principal amount for each full month (30 days) remaining until maturity.
  </p>

   </em>

However, you'll need to compensate for the remaining cost of the Etihad Guest Miles you've received, calculated as 0.5% of the principal amount for each full month (30 days) remaining until maturity. The rest of the principal amount will be moved to your current account.
   <br/>
   <br/>
   <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
    Let's take an example:
   </strong>

   </li>
   <li class="ghq-card-content__bulleted-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="BULLETED_LIST_ITEM">

   </li>
   <li class="ghq-card-content__bulleted-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="BULLETED_LIST_ITEM">

   <li class="ghq-card-content__bulleted-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="BULLETED_LIST_ITEM">

   </li>
   <li class="ghq-card-content__bulleted-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="BULLETED_LIST_ITEM">

   </li>
   <li class="ghq-card-content__bulleted-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="BULLETED_LIST_ITEM">
    The 100,000 Etihad Guest Miles would remain in your Etihad Account with no changes or limitations
   </li>
  </ul>
 </div>
</details>
