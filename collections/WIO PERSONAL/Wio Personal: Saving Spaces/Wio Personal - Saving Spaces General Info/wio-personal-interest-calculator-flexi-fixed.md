 </strong>
 Interest on the Fixed Saving Space Account is accrued daily and paid upon maturity, with the rate remaining unchanged throughout the deposit term.
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph">
 No Automatic Rollover:¬†At maturity, the Fixed Saving Space does not automatically renew, and will cease to accrue interest.

 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  No Mid-Term Contributions:
 </strong>
 Additional funds cannot be added to the Fixed Saving Space Account for the duration of the deposit term.
