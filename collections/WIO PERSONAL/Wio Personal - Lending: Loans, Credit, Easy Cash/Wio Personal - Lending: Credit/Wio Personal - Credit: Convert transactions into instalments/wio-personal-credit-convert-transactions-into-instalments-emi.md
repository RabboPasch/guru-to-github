<h2 class="ghq-card-content__medium-heading" data-ghq-card-content-type="MEDIUM_HEADING" id="N7lGO79tjucd">
 How Does It Work?
</h2>
<ol class="ghq-card-content__numbered-list" data-ghq-card-content-type="NUMBERED_LIST" start="1">
 <li class="ghq-card-content__numbered-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="NUMBERED_LIST_ITEM" id="ZKT7gsZnprl0">
  <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
   Eligibility
  </strong>
  : To qualify for the instalment option, your credit purchase must be settled, exceeds AED 1,000 and be made within 60 days from the settlement date, cash-related transactions (such as easy cash) are not eligible for credit instalments
 </li>
 <li class="ghq-card-content__numbered-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="NUMBERED_LIST_ITEM" id="xV9yORtlNFG3">
  <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
   Foreign currency installment support:
  </strong>
  Non-AED transactions exceeding the value or AED 1,000 are eligible for credit installments. When customers make an international transaction, it can also be converted to installments.
 </li>
 <li class="ghq-card-content__numbered-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="NUMBERED_LIST_ITEM" id="b1YHnvrIAXN9">
  <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
   Apply for instalments
  </strong>
  : Simply navigate to the eligible transaction list in the app, select your transaction, and click "Apply for instalment."
 </li>
 <li class="ghq-card-content__numbered-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="NUMBERED_LIST_ITEM" id="lTFxvd8J4tZT">
  <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
   Fee Structure
  </strong>
  : Fees vary from 0% for a 3-month plan to between 14.06% and 16.76% for longer terms.
 </li>
 <li class="ghq-card-content__numbered-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="NUMBERED_LIST_ITEM" id="quZvTxR5lNFl">
  <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
   Review and Confirm
  </strong>
  : On the instalment details page, you can choose the number of instalments, review the fees, and see the total repayment amount before confirming your plan.
 </li>
 <li class="ghq-card-content__numbered-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="NUMBERED_LIST_ITEM" id="L5trvfnFxMDn">
  <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
   Automatic Payments
  </strong>
  : Monthly payments will be automatically deducted from your account on the due date, making it hassle-free for you.
 </li>
 <li class="ghq-card-content__numbered-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="NUMBERED_LIST_ITEM" id="Jfz8HP3KbEr0">
  <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
   Next Cycle Pay Option
  </strong>
  : If you initiate your instalment plan within 7 days before your due date, we will shift your first instalment to the next month's due date.
 </li>
 <li class="ghq-card-content__numbered-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="NUMBERED_LIST_ITEM" id="j71B1uchaLxL">
  <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
   Due Date Policy
  </strong>
  : The due date and repayment date will remain the same as your Wio Credit due date; however, there will be a separate cut-off time. The normal credit auto payment due date is 9 PM on the due date, while the credit installment autopayment cut-off time is 10:00 PM on the due date.
 </li>
</ol>

        Example
       </strong>
       : If you borrow AED 100,000 at a flat rate of 5% per annum for 2 years, the interest is calculated as 5% of AED 100,000 each year. So, the total interest over 2 years would be AED 10,000 (5% of 100,000 * 2 years).
      </p>
     </td>
     <td class="ghq-card-content__table-cell" data-ghq-card-content-type="TABLE_CELL">
      <p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph">
       <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
        Definition
       </strong>
       : The interest is calculated on the outstanding principal amount, which decreases as you make repayments.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
        Example
       </strong>
       : If you borrow AED 100,000 at a reducing balance rate of 5% per annum for 2 years, the interest for the first month is calculated on AED 100,000. As you repay the principal, the interest for subsequent months is calculated on the reduced principal amount. This results in lower interest payments over time compared to the flat rate.
      </p>
     </td>
    </tr>
   </tbody>
  </table>
 </div>
</div>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="wZnWJJxdXD1N">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  Key Differences
 </strong>
</p>
<ul class="ghq-card-content__bulleted-list" data-ghq-card-content-type="BULLETED_LIST">
 <li class="ghq-card-content__bulleted-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="BULLETED_LIST_ITEM" id="MkcljZW1TGzd">
  <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
   Interest Calculation
  </strong>
  : Flat rate calculates interest on the full principal amount throughout the loan term, while reducing balance calculates interest on the remaining principal.
 </li>
 <li class="ghq-card-content__bulleted-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="BULLETED_LIST_ITEM" id="jR95NAl0ghDO">
  <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
   Cost
  </strong>
  : Reducing balance method generally results in lower total interest paid over the loan term compared to the flat rate method.
 </li>
</ul>

 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="FFLrnD4M2om2">
 Purchases above AED 1,000 can be converted into instalments, subject to eligibility criteria.

<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="oVmZnFLDfbvX">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   3. What tenures are available for instalment plans?
  </span>
 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="BVNfX73HDL9h">
 You can choose repayment tenures of 3, 6, 12, 18, 24, 36, or 48 months, depending on the transaction's eligibility.
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="I7JHT5HBSHAj">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   4. What is the interest rate for the instalment plan?
  </span>
 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="z77777xRhxoG">
 The interest rate varies based on tenure and promotion. Some plans offer
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  0% interest
 </strong>
 , while others have a
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  fixed interest rate
 </strong>
 disclosed at the time of conversion.
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="3DdCjP4dVmxt">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   5. Does converting a transaction into instalments reduce my credit limit?
  </span>
 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="jPpvDFWsZALL">
 Yes, the total instalment amount is deducted from your
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  available credit limit
 </strong>
 and gets restored gradually as you repay each instalment.
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="HHdw4pVLbIHr">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   6. What happens if I miss an instalment payment?
  </span>
 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="rK53GTvtXtv1">
 Missing a payment may result in a
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  late payment fee
 </strong>
 , additional interest charges, and a possible impact on your
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  credit score
 </strong>
 .
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="9FVrx1xvZANF">
 Installment delinquency is combined with credit card delinquency: If the customer didn't pay one or multiple installments, it will be reflected as credit card delinquency. Late fee and DPD calculation is same as credit card.
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="HwbXxhZoYq58">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">

 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="hrJzX3Npx0Ij">
 You can visit the 'Spend' section to view all eligible transactions. Select the transaction you wish to convert and review the instalment details before proceeding with the conversion..
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="qz0JpB7EhPRj">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   8. Will I still earn rewards or cashback on instalment transactions?
  </span>
 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="vbmAAN91SJfh">
 Yes, you will continue to earn rewards or cashback on instalment transactions.
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="HVj9vZPgtSFL">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   9. What happens if I return a purchase that I converted into instalments?
  </span>
 </strong>
 <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
 </span>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="u94Fhm15Bcsk">
 If you return a purchase, the refunded amount will be credited back to your account; however, the instalment plan will remain active, including any accrued interest.

<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="UTfPFCPfrNrS">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">

  </span>
 </strong>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" id="nRQuYFfDpfiL">

 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  credit limit
 </strong>
 and meet eligibility criteria.
</p>
