 Topic Overview

<h2 class="ghq-card-content__medium-heading" data-ghq-card-content-type="MEDIUM_HEADING">
 Repayment Schedule
</h2>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph">
 The maximum amount customer can get as Easy Cash is 50% of Wio Credit limit up to a specified limit. The limit is combined with Wio credit limit. the borrowed amount must be repaid in full within 30 days from the first borrowing.
</p>
<hr class="ghq-card-content__horizontal-rule" data-ghq-card-content-type="DIVIDER"/>
<h2 class="ghq-card-content__medium-heading" data-ghq-card-content-type="MEDIUM_HEADING">
 Fee Calculation
</h2>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph">
 There are two types of fees: interest and late payment fee.
</p>
<ul class="ghq-card-content__bulleted-list" data-ghq-card-content-type="BULLETED_LIST">
 <li class="ghq-card-content__bulleted-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="BULLETED_LIST_ITEM">
  Interest is applied daily, and charged at the time customer pay manually or autopay daily. It is calculated based on the outstanding principal
  <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  </strong>
  daily and will accumulate. The calculation method is fee rate multiple outstanding.
 </li>
</ul>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph align-center" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph" data-text-align="center">
 <span class="ghq-card-content__image-container">
  <img alt="image.png" class="ghq-card-content__image" data-ghq-card-content-image-filename="image.png" data-ghq-card-content-type="IMAGE" src="/collections/WIO PERSONAL/resources/image.png" style="width:664px" width="664"/>
 </span>
</p>
<ul class="ghq-card-content__bulleted-list" data-ghq-card-content-type="BULLETED_LIST">
 <li class="ghq-card-content__bulleted-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="BULLETED_LIST_ITEM">
  Late payment fee is a flat fee of AED [199] (incl. VAT), will be charged at T+6, including holidays.
 </li>
 <li class="ghq-card-content__bulleted-list-item" data-ghq-card-content-type="BULLETED_LIST_ITEM">
  Disbursement (paying out) can only be multiples of AED 1,000.
 </li>
</ul>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph">
 <span class="ghq-card-content__image-container">

 </span>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  Repayment hierarchy is interest &gt; principle &gt; late
 </strong>
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  payment fee.
