<h1 class="ghq-card-content__large-heading" data-ghq-card-content-type="LARGE_HEADING">
 10th Feb 2025 - Chimera iBoxx US Treasury Bill ETF
</h1>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph">
 The Chimera iBoxx US Treasury Bill ETF, which will be listed on the ADX, is an exchange-traded fund that aims to track and replicate the performance of the iBoxx USD Treasury Bills Index, reflecting the returns of USD-denominated Treasury bills issued by the US Government (prospectus attached).
 <br/>
</p>
<p class="ghq-card-content__paragraph" data-ghq-card-content-type="paragraph">
 <strong class="ghq-card-content__bold" data-ghq-card-content-type="BOLD">
  <span class="ghq-card-content__text-color" data-ghq-card-content-type="TEXT_COLOR" style="color:#9013fe">
   Important:
  </span>
 </strong>
 This is an Exchange Traded Fund, which will be listed on ADX. This is not an IPO, so it is likely you will receive a full allocation of the amount you have subscribed for during the Initial Offer Period.
 <br/>
</p>
